Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 26/05/2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

Our GPS Measure app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app functions fully without requiring any user information.

2. Location Access

The app uses your device's GPS solely to measure distances between two geographic points. This location data is processed locally and never transmitted or stored externally.

3. No Internet Dependency

The app does not require an internet connection. All features are available offline and the app does not connect to any external servers.

4. Data Storage

All data generated (e.g., coordinates, measurements) is handled within your device. We do not store or share any of your location or measurement history.

5. Security

Your privacy and data security are important. The app ensures your location data is not accessible by any third party or cloud service.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this Privacy Policy will reflect new features or changes in functionality. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
